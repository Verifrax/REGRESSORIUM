# REGRESSORIUM Charter

REGRESSORIUM exists so that no recognized truth leaves consequence floating, ownerless, unevidenced, or procedurally unresolved.

## System axiom

```text
No terminal consequence may be assigned without terminal recognition.
No terminal recognition may be mistaken for terminal recourse.
```

## Repository doctrine

REGRESSORIUM owns recourse-of-record.

It holds the burden assignment, claim posture, remedy path, escalation path, and closure rules that follow from recognized truth.
It does not own terminal recognition itself.
